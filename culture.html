Environmental

  • Cavendish banana is the most produced and marketed banana.
  • Grown in massive quantities in the tropical regions of the world.
  • Strain of Fusarium wilt (Tropical Race Four) is spreading across banana belt leading to the death of many Cavendish banana plants.
  • Epidemic already seen in loss of the Gros Michel banana to Fusarium oxysporum.
  • Massive deforestation for banana boom in 80s and 90s.


    • Ethical

  • Destruction of rainforest for land used for banana plantations.
  • After drive in demand for bananas in 80s and 90s.
  • Many natural rainforests have been deforested for bananas.
  • Ethical issue involving destroying forests for food for people or keeping forests intact.

    • Social

  • Massive boom in banana industry in 80s and 90s.
  • Boom followed by decline in banana market.
  • Loss of jobs by many people in tropic nations.
  • Brought some families to extreme poverty.

    • Economic

  • Massive cash crop for the tropics.
  • Environmental issues that are affecting bananas trickle down into the economy.
  • Major loss of exports for these small countries in the tropics.
  • Along with loss of exports comes fall of businesses and loss of jobs.
